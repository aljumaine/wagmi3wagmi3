Glosaary of Terms:

DEX: Decentralized exchange. Cryptocurrency exchange which allows for direct 1-to-1 cryptocurrency transactions. Securely and without any 3 side. ( Government, Business owner, etc..)

Token: Virtual currency, tradable asset or some kind of utility. Example: Online games have their own tokens to buy virtual assets inside the game.

Altcoin: Any kind of cryptocurrency other than Bitcoin. Etherium for example. All the projects in the Crypto world have their own Altcoin (Token) with some kind of utility & value.

Staking: A way to put your crypto to work. Like a "Deposit" in the bank. Rewards will be new Crypto tokens. It's a process that involves your crypto to support a blockchain network and confirm transactions. % from Staking can be 2% or 500% Depending on the project stage

 

Yield Farming: Lending your cryptocurrency for the liquidity process in DEX (Exchanges) platforms. In return, you get interest and sometimes fees. % of Yield Farming can be 1000% per year. **This is a risky way to make money** Note: Yield farming and staking %% Returns in general seem to be higher when thereâ€™s more risk involved, so people should be cautious when approaching such ventures with high risk.

Stable Coin: Stablecoins are cryptocurrencies that attempt to hold their market price to something external. To a currency like the U.S. dollar or to Gold.

IDO: Initial DEX offering. New crypto project launch on Crowdfunding platforms. If you hear on Twitter about 1000% growth investments. Probably it's an IDO.

Vesting: Process of locking and releasing tokens after some time on an early stage of a project. Example: After IDO launch. This way price of IDO tokens doesn't go extremely down.

NFT: Non-fungible token. Can be a Photo, video, audio, and any other digital file linked to the blockchain. Based on NFT tech. you can have public proof of ownership on a photo of your cat.

Minting NFT refers to the process of publishing a digital file on the Ethereum blockchain, turning it into a digital asset, in this process you pay gas fees.

Whitelisting: Being eligible to participate in some IDO launch or NFT Presale collection.

Airdrop: Give-away that involves sending coins or tokens to your Crypto wallet. It can be because you holding or staking tokens of some new project. Or because of your promotions actions that projects asked you to do. Anyway, it can be a nice bonus.
